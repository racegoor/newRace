Race

- the game that takes you as far as possible!!!

Race is a very exciting game developed by Zipi & Zipi as part of final project of computer programing. the idea of the game came after Zipi A wanted to develop an application with Google Maps while Zipi B prefered to develop a game. The two friends tried to reach an agreement which ultimately produced this creative game. Game on Google's panorama is exciting and challenging. You can drive on familiar streets, from your home to friends or to school, and maybe even try to reach faraway foreign places. When was the last time you visited the Big Ben in London or toured the streets of Paris? Here you will be able to feel as if you are in the place you chose and you will get to know it better then its residents!
